Condensed Milk Cinnamon Iced Coffee

  • 1.5 tsp instant coffee
  • 1.5-2 tsp sweetened condensed milk
  • cinnamon powder (serving size as desired)
  • 30 ml hot water
  • 125-150 ml oatmilk
  • ice (serving size as desired)
  • optional: milk frother
  1. In any preferred cup, pour in the instant coffee, cinnamon, sweetened condensed milk, and hot water.
  2. Use a milk frother to mix the ingredients together. If a milk frother is unavailable then use any utensil, like a spoon or small whisk, to do the mixing.
  3. Once the ingredients are well-mixed, add ice to liking.
  4. Finish it off by pouring in the oatmilk.
  5. Enjoy!
